RAG Survey: Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Large Language Models

The most comprehensive academic survey of RAG. Covers Naive, Advanced, and Modular RAG with evaluation benchmarks. The go-to reference paper for understanding the full landscape.

Building and Evaluating Advanced RAG
